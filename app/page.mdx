# Build Your AI Staff with Claude Code

**A free course for coaches and content creators who want to leverage AI agents to scale their business.**

---

## What You'll Learn

This course teaches you how to build a complete "staff" of AI agents using Claude Code — the most powerful way to work with AI for real business tasks.

By the end, you'll have:

- **Executive Assistant Agent** — Email drafting, research, scheduling prep
- **Customer Success Agent** — Support responses, FAQ management, onboarding
- **Audience Intelligence Agent** — ICP research, competitive analysis, trend monitoring
- **Content Ecosystem** — Strategy + creation agents working together
- **Strategic Agents** — Marketing and decision-making support
- **Full Orchestration** — Agents that direct other agents

---

## Who This Is For

This course is designed for **coaches and content creators** who:

- Want to scale their business without hiring (yet)
- Are comfortable following technical instructions (no coding required)
- Have a Claude Pro or Max subscription ($20/month)
- Want practical, immediately useful AI workflows

---

## How It Works

Unlike typical courses, this one is **interactive**. You'll:

1. Download the starter repo
2. Work directly in Claude Code while following along
3. Build your actual agent staff as you learn
4. Leave with working systems, not just theory
